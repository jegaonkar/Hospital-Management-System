Shwas Hospital started in 2009 and is private limited hospital under ownership of ‘Shwas Hospital & Research Center Dharashiv’. Hospital was inaugurated by Late Hon. Vilasraoji Deshmukh Shwas Hospital is situated in the heart of the Dharashiv city.Shwas Hospital & Research Center Dharashiv Superspeciality Hospital is a multi speciality hospital with 65 beds capacity, which provides the complete range of outpatients and inpatients.

Patient comfort is the core tenant of the hospital, where we treat every patient with utmost priority.
